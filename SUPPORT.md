## Frequently Asked Questions

### Adding Shows

**How do I add a concert?**
Tap the + button on the Shows tab to add manually, or use the calendar icon to scan your calendar for past shows. You can also share a ticket email or screenshot directly to Moshed from Mail or Photos.

**Can I import from Ticketmaster or Dice?**
Yes — forward your confirmation email to the Mail app, then tap Share → Moshed. Or scan your calendar if the event was saved there automatically.

**Moshed added a duplicate show — what do I do?**
Moshed checks for duplicates automatically, but if one slips through you can delete it by swiping left on the show in the Shows tab.

### Subscriptions

**What does Moshed+ include?**
Unlimited photos per show, advanced stats, Year in Review, artist genre backfill, and CSV/PDF export.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Moshed → Cancel. You keep access until the end of the billing period.

**Can I get a refund?**
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) to request one.

**I subscribed but Moshed+ isn't active — what do I do?**
Go to Settings inside Moshed and tap Restore Purchases.

### Data & Privacy

**Is my data backed up?**
Yes — if you have iCloud backup enabled on your device, your Moshed data is included automatically.

**Will I lose my data if I delete the app?**
If iCloud backup is on, reinstalling the app will restore your data. Without iCloud backup, data cannot be recovered after deletion.

**Does Moshed collect any personal data?**
No. See our [Privacy Policy](https://venetsia.github.io/moshed-support/privacy) for full details.

### General

**Which iOS version does Moshed require?**
iOS 17 or later.

**Is Moshed available on iPad?**
Moshed is designed for iPhone. It will run on iPad but is not optimised for it yet.
