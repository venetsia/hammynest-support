# HammyNest — The Complete Hamster Care Companion

Everything you need to give your hamster the best life possible. Built by a hamster owner, for hamster owners.

## Features

**Daily Care**
- Feeding tracker with smart food suggestions
- Cage cleaning log with reminders
- Daily care checklist
- Playtime timer for free-roam sessions

**Health & Wellness**
- Health journal with weight tracking
- Mood and behaviour logging
- Sleep pattern analysis
- Heat cycle & scent tracker
- Care score report card

**Guides & Reference**
- Food safety database — 91 foods rated safe, caution, or dangerous
- Emergency guide for health crises
- Behaviour guide — understand what your hamster is doing
- Breed encyclopedia for all 5 breeds
- Cage setup checklist

**Premium**
- Unlimited hamster profiles
- Full food database with breed-specific portions
- Health journal, mood tracker, and sleep analysis
- Photo gallery and journal
- Care calendar, expense tracker, and more
- 7-day free trial

## Privacy

HammyNest does not collect any personal data. All your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We never see, access, or share your information.

## Disclaimer

HammyNest provides general educational information about hamster care. This app is not a substitute for professional veterinary advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified exotic animal veterinarian if you have any concerns about your hamster's health.
