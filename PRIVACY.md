# HammyNest — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

## Overview

HammyNest is developed by an independent developer based in Scotland. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how HammyNest handles your information — the short version is: we don't collect any.

## Data We Collect

None. HammyNest does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. We do not operate any servers or databases. We have no ability to access your data.

## Data You Create

When you use HammyNest, you create data such as hamster profiles, feeding logs, health records, photos, journal entries, and other care information. This data is stored:

- Locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
- In your personal iCloud account (if iCloud is enabled) via Apple's CloudKit

This data belongs to you. We cannot see, access, read, or export it.

## Third-Party Services

HammyNest does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, social media, or tracking services. The only external services used are Apple's own frameworks:

- **StoreKit** — for processing in-app purchases (handled entirely by Apple)
- **CloudKit** — for iCloud sync (data stored in YOUR iCloud account)
- **UserNotifications** — for local reminders (scheduled on-device, no external server)

## In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. HammyNest never sees your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID credentials. We only receive confirmation of whether a valid subscription exists.

## Analytics & Tracking

HammyNest does not use any analytics or tracking tools. We do not collect usage data, device information, IP addresses, or any form of telemetry. We do not use advertising identifiers. App Tracking Transparency does not apply because we do not track users.

## Children's Privacy

HammyNest does not knowingly collect information from children. Since we collect no data from any users, this applies universally.

## Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account, you have full control:

- Delete individual records within the app at any time
- Use the "Delete All My Data" option in Settings > Privacy & Data
- Delete the app to remove all local data
- Manage iCloud data via Settings > Apple ID > iCloud on your device

## GDPR Compliance (EU/UK)

HammyNest is inherently compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK GDPR because:

- We do not collect or process personal data
- All data remains under your exclusive control
- You can access, export, and delete your data at any time
- No data is transferred to third parties
- No data leaves your device/iCloud account

## CCPA Compliance (California)

HammyNest does not sell, share, or disclose personal information. Since we collect no data, the California Consumer Privacy Act's requirements regarding disclosure, opt-out, and deletion are met by default.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
